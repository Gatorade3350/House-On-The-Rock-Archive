Contribute Research Materials

Help expand this research archive by contributing historical materials, documents, or research about the House on the Rock!

We're looking for:

  • Historical photographs and documents
  • Academic papers or theses about the property
  • Old tour maps, brochures, or promotional materials
  • Audio recordings of the music machines
  • Personal accounts from visits or work at the House
  • Research on Alex Jordan Jr. and his vision
  • Information about specific music machines or collections
Important: All contributions will be properly attributed and credited. Please ensure you have permission to share any materials, especially photographs or copyrighted content.

How to contribute: Submit materials through the GitHub repository or contact us directly. We'll review all submissions and integrate appropriate materials into the archive.

Research Notes & Caveats

Machine Playability: It is unknown exactly which instruments throughout the House are actually being played while you are there, and which have been recorded and are only played through hidden speakers. These music machines are primarily show pieces and are not carefully maintained for ongoing playability.
Evolving Collection: The House on the Rock's collection is constantly changing. Machines may be moved, restored, or replaced over time. This archive represents a snapshot based on available documentation.
