# House on the Rock Music Machines Guide

A modern, performant web guide to the music machines at the House on the Rock attraction in Spring Green, Wisconsin.

This website is being produced by a current HOTR employee and (currently) is not endorced by the company, so i am doing this fully out of my own love of this beautiful complex.

The House on the Rock was started in the early to late 50's, following Alex Jordan's ever growing need to escape the city of madison. when he opend the house, it was a whole 50 cents to tour the house. Today the curernt price is 36.95$ for over 2.5 miles of complex, that have been built up by Mr. Jordan. 

So this site is made as a bridge between what you can see at the house, and some explination of how the complex came to be, and how he collected all his displays.
